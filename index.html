TravelWiz

Foodylytics

Eat healthier and spice up your life with Foodylytics. Foodylytics is a phone application design catered towards the elderly with a primary focus of promoting health. Foodylytics achieves this by designing an app that allows elderlies dietary information based on their dietary restrictions; what food to avoid. It also can recommend a variety of healthy food options that is within their restrictions, making it so that the elderly will never grow bored eating the same food all the time, and potential to explore different types of food as well, contributing to a healthier and enriching life. Check out Foodylytics.

TeamUp!

TeamUp! Is a web application that tackles the problem of group and collaboration creation in a classroom setting. This is done by giving the users more power to create or join projects they believe in. This also allows users to find the best possible team as well, since people are joining ideas they are interested in, groupmates will put effort to make the ideas realized. Great team makes great products, and TeamUp gives users, opportunity to create such teams by allowing them to view who signed up for their idea, along with allowing certain requirements to request to join, which in turn may create a good synergy with other groupmates. Check out TeamUp!

Spotify Top Song (Data Science Project)

This is a data science group project for the class COGS 108, which was to use various data science skills learned in class to gather data and conduct a research project. The question our group decided to target was “What factors makes a song popular?” We decided to use Spotify top 50 charts and gathered data using Spotify api and kworb.net, which is a site that aggregates Spotify Data and create a chart from the data. Factors that we decided on could range from tone, amplitude, notes, and even artist. After gathering data we created various charts to show our findings. Check out Data Science Project.
